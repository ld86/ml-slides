Tf-Idf - :

  • Term frequency $$tf(t, d) = \log n_{t,d}$$

  • Inverse Document frequency $$idf(t, D) = \log \frac{ \mid D \mid}{\mid \{ d_i \in D \mid t \in d_i \} \mid}$$

  • Tf-Idf tf idf $$ TfIdf(t, d, D) = tf(t, d) * idf(t, D)$$

BM25¶

Okapi BM25 Tf-Idf, $tf(t, d)$.

$$tf(t, d) = \frac{(K_1 + 1) * n_{t,d}}{k_1 * (1 - b + b * \frac{L_d}{L_{ave}}) + n_{t,d}}$$

  • $L_{ave}$
  • $1.2 \leqslant k \leqslant 2$
  • $b = 0.75$
